TrueSight DAO DApp

Web-based tools for TrueSight DAO members to manage cacao inventory, scan QR codes, and handle voting rights.

Identity & Governance

Digital Signature Creator

Create a secure digital identity for DAO tasks.

Features:

  • Generate and store a key pair
  • Register your identity with the DAO
  • Authenticate for other tools

Usage:

  1. Open Digital Signature Creator.
  2. Generate and save your keys.
  3. Share your public key to our Telegram channel.

Voting Rights Cash Out

Trade your voting rights for assets.

Features:

  • View voting rights balance
  • Submit signed withdrawal requests
  • Share requests for approval

Usage:

  1. Open Voting Rights Cash Out.
  2. Enter amount and price.
  3. Submit and share request to our Telegram channel.

Withdrawal Request Verifier

Check withdrawal request authenticity.

Features:

  • Verify digital signatures
  • Confirm member and transaction details

Usage:

  1. Open Verifier.
  2. Paste request text.
  3. Check authenticity.

Inventory & Sales

Cacao Bag Scanner

Scan QR codes to report cacao sales.

Features:

  • Scan bag QR codes
  • Share sale reports

Usage:

  1. Open Cacao Bag Scanner.
  2. Scan QR code.
  3. Share sale report to our Telegram channel.

Inventory Expense Reporter

Report DAO inventory expenses.

Features:

  • Select resources and quantities
  • Attach proof (image/PDF)
  • Share expense reports

Usage:

  1. Open Expense Reporter.
  2. Select resource and quantity.
  3. Submit report with proof to our Telegram channel.

Inventory Movement Reporter

Track inventory transfers.

Features:

  • Select items and recipients
  • Capture snapshots
  • Share transfer reports

Usage:

  1. Open Movement Reporter.
  2. Select item and recipient.
  3. Submit report with snapshot to our Telegram channel.

Future Modules

  • Contributions reporting for $TDG awards
  • Voting rights marketplace
  • DAO governance proposal system
